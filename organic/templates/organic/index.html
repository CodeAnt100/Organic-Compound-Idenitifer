Welcome to the organic identification program!


This program allows you to input a structural formula for an organic compound and outputs its IUPAC name.
The types of functional groups and organic compounds this program can identify include:
  • Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes (including compounds with multiple double and triple bonds)
  • Carboxylic Acids (COOH), Nitriles (CN), Aldehydes (CHO), Ketones (COC), Alcohols (OH) and Amines (NH2)
  • Compounds with multiple functional groups in different positions and with correct precedence naming
  • Branched compounds containing Fluorine (F), Iodine (I), Bromine (Br), Chlorine (Cl) and Alkyls (CH3, C2H5, etc.)
N.B. The program does not accept condensed structural formulae (e.g. CH3(CH2)5CH3)
